Proactive engineer keeping ISP and enterprise networks online — across LAN, WAN, VPN, FTTH and beyond.

I'm a Technical Support Engineer currently at Vilcom Networks, with over two years of hands-on experience in NOC operations, incident response, and customer-impacting troubleshooting across ISP and enterprise environments. I work shifts on a 24/7 rotation, respond to escalations, and own service availability for real customers.

My day-to-day spans LAN/WAN, VPN, wireless, FTTH and routing — diagnosing issues, restoring service within SLA, performing root cause analysis, and feeding lessons back into documentation. I have a strong working understanding of TCP/IP, routing & switching, DNS, DHCP, and network security fundamentals, plus tools like Wireshark and SolarWinds.

On the software side, I'm ALX-certified in Software Engineering — I write Python and JavaScript, build internal tooling, and treat automation as the natural next step beyond manual troubleshooting. I'm actively pursuing CCNA and CompTIA Security+ to deepen my edge in secure network operations.
